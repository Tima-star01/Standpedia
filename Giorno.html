Giorno Giovanna

Gold Experience Requiem

Giorno is not just a JoJo — he is a symbol of ambition, logic, and vision. Though born from DIO’s bloodline, his spirit is that of a true Joestar: kind, unyielding, and strategic.

With his Stand, Gold Experience, he can give life to anything: transform stones into snakes, objects into creatures, or even blood into flesh.

But when his Stand evolves into Gold Experience Requiem, he transcends logic. This power cancels any action, turning it to zero. Attack? Blocked. Death? Undone. Victory? Never granted.

Giorno doesn't just fight — he rewrites reality.
