Jonathan Joestar

Ripple (Hamon) Master

Jonathan Joestar is the first JoJo — a noble gentleman, strong both in body and soul. He doesn’t possess a Stand, but masters the ancient technique of Ripple (Hamon), a breathing-based energy that channels the power of the sun through the user's body.

Technique: Hamon (Ripple)
Effects: Destroys vampires, heals wounds, strengthens physical ability
Training: Learned from Master Zeppeli through harsh discipline

Ripple energy flows through Jonathan's attacks with radiant power. His fists aren’t just powerful — they’re sacred.

“I will defeat evil with the power of the Ripple — and the strength of my heart.”
