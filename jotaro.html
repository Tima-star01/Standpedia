Jotaro Kujo

Star Platinum: The World

Jotaro Kujo is the grandson of Joseph Joestar and one of the most iconic Stand users in history. A stoic genius, half-Japanese, half-European, he rarely speaks — but when he does, it's final.

Stand: Star Platinum
A Stand of immense strength, speed, and precision. In time, it awakened the ability to stop time for several seconds, allowing Jotaro to strike before anyone could react.
Battle IQ:
Unlike his brash grandfather, Jotaro uses pure logic, observation, and deception. He analyzes enemy habits, manipulates tempo, and never reveals his full hand until it matters.
Legacy:
After defeating DIO — the immortal vampire — Jotaro not only saved the world, but revived his grandfather by destroying the source of Dio's parasitic control. This fight became legend — a true Joestar victory across generations.
