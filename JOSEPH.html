Joseph Joestar

Hermit Purple • Ripple Genius

Grandson of Jonathan Joestar, Joseph inherits the Joestar will — but expresses it in a completely different way. Unlike his noble and disciplined grandfather, Joseph is unpredictable, cunning, sarcastic, and wildly clever.

Stand: Hermit Purple
Hermit Purple is a vine-like Stand that manifests from Joseph’s hands. It allows for psychic photographs (Spirit Photography), long-range energy transmission, and even movement via grappling.
Ability: Ripple (Hamon)
Joseph is possibly the last living master of Ripple. His Hamon skills are creative and chaotic — using bottlecaps, clackers, even a pair of headphones as Hamon conductors. His mastery proves how far Ripple can evolve in the hands of a genius.
Mind & Tactics:
Joseph’s greatest weapon is his intelligence and intuition. He can predict enemy behavior, foresee what others will say, and craft plans within seconds. His signature move: "Your next line is..." — is more than a catchphrase. It’s a psychological blow.
Philosophy of Combat:
Despite his wild nature, Joseph respects his enemies and mourns the fall of worthy opponents. He believes in tactical survival over blind bravery. He created the legendary "Plan B": when the situation turns bad — run, regroup, and outsmart!
