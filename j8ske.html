Josuke Higashikata

Crazy Diamond

Josuke is the fourth JoJo — a teenager with style, attitude, and a heart of gold. He’s laid-back with friends, soft with women, but will explode with fury if you dare mock his hair. His hairstyle is a tribute to the mysterious man who saved his life when he was critically ill.

Stand: Crazy Diamond
Crazy Diamond has the power to restore anything it touches — objects, people, even broken hearts. It can also use destruction tactically: break a wall, repair it instantly to trap enemies inside. But Josuke cannot heal himself.
Character:
Josuke loves fashion, has a great sense of justice, and defends his town with fierce loyalty. Although he often acts silly or emotional, he’s highly resourceful and surprisingly clever in battle. And yes — canon confirms: he literally doesn’t see well.
